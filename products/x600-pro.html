CUTTING-EDGE PERFORMANCE

The X600 Pro never comes up short with its Intel® 11th gen i5/i7/i9 processor (up to 8 cores), optional NVIDIA® Quadro® RTX3000 discrete graphics controller, and up to 128GB DDR4 RAM available. Its dual-fan design keeps each of these computing components cool and capable, even during the most computationally-intensive tasks, while data storage can include up to three user-removable PCIe solid-state drives (SSDs), totaling 6TB.

SUPERB USER EXPERIENCE

The X600 Pro’s Windows 11 Pro operating system (OS) enables an intuitively familiar core Microsoft user experience, but with enhanced hybrid/remote work functionality, and easier management of multiple tasks. All made easier still by X600 Pro’s spacious 15.6” LumiBond® screen, which delivers superb color and contrast, sunlight-readable brightness (1,000 nits), and optional capacitive multitouch screen capability, that's operable in the presence of cold, rain, or when used with gloves.

BUILT FOR EXPANSION

The X600 Pro is engineered with hardware expansion in mind. With its optional second and third user-removable solid-state drives it can reach a capacious 6TB. Dual card readers (ExpressCard and PCMCIA) are also available. With its onboard display port, HDMI and Thunderbolt™ 4 connectors, it can power multiple monitors simultaneously. And with its multimedia bay, which can house an optional DVD/Blu-ray drive, (or two additional batteries), X600 Pro is designed for uncompromised productivity.
