The V110 is built to perform, featuring a powerful 10th generation Intel® Core™ processor, flash storage and responsive graphics. It’s the fastest rugged laptop we’ve ever built. A compact and lightweight laptop, it quickly transforms from 11.6 inch display notebook to tablet computer.

LumiBond® 2.0 - Bright & Tough

The V110 with our LumiBond® 2.0 touchscreen with bonded display glass to the touch panel and LCD, creates a durable, single pane that is more readable. The hard-tip stylus enables signature capture and taking more precise notes on maps or forms.

Built To Survive™

The V110’s main chassis structure is precision cast using Magnesium Alloy, an incredibly strong structural metal that is one of the lightest in the world. The V110 has been independently tested and certified to MIL-STD-810H, IP65 and MIL-STD461G.
