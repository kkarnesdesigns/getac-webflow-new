MISSION-READY COMPUTING, BUILT FOR EXTREMES

Designed for defense, the B360 Pro fully rugged laptop is purpose-built to perform in challenging tactical environments. Powered by a 12th-gen Intel Core™ processor with integrated Intel Iris and up to 64GB DDR4 RAM, and an optional NVIDIA GTX 1650 graphics, the B360 Pro delivers extraordinary performance for ultra-fast map display, video playback and device responsiveness under heavy workloads. The B360 Pro has up to 3 SSDs (with an option of up to 6TB storage), offering ample storage; it can also support up to 3 batteries to ensure maximized productivity.

HIGHER PORTABILITY THAT WEIGHS LESS

Starting at 3.08kg (6.79 lbs), the B360 Pro delivers exceptional rugged flagship mobility that makes it easy to carry on foot, store in a compartment, or mount safely in a vehicle, where its rounded corners and simple docking connection, featuring I/O port replication, make in-cabin use safe and convenient.

SECURED THROUGHOUT

The B360 Pro goes beyond essential when it comes to privacy and data security. The device enables quick and safe sign-in through one or more multi-factor authentications, including a smart card reader, an optional HF RFID reader, and face authentication via an optional Windows Hello. Getac Disk Wipe solidifies the permanent irretrievability of stored data, safeguarding your data from reaching unintended or inappropriate parties. Dual user-removable storage allows for easy removal of classified hard drives knowing data is safely secured back at command. Command is synced with the B360 Pro, with full access to and complete control over sensitive data.
