CUTTING-EDGE PERFORMANCE

The X600 never comes up short with its Intel 11th gen i5/i7/i9 processor, (up to 8 cores), optional NVIDIA® Quadro® RTX3000 discrete graphics controller, and up to 128GB DDR4 RAM available. Its dual-fan design keeps each of these computing components cool and capable, even during the most intensive tasks, while data storage can be expanded up to 6TB with three user-removable PCIe solid-state drives (SSDs).

SUPERB USER EXPERIENCE

The X600’s Windows 11 Pro operating system (OS) enables an intuitively familiar Microsoft user experience, but with enhanced hybrid/remote work functionality, and easier management of multiple tasks. And with X600’s spacious 15.6” LumiBond® screen, which delivers superb color and contrast, sunlight-readable brightness (1,000 nits), and optional capacitive multitouch screen capability, operable in the presence of cold, rain, or when used with gloves, multitasking is easier even in a difficult environment.

BUILT FOR MOBILITY

At less than two inches thick, X600 is built for mobility in a vehicle, (custom mount available), or on foot. And with its dual hot-swappable batteries - each able to be changed out toollessly without interrupting device operation, X600 can work all day without the need to plug in.
