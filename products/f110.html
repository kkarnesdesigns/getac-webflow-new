COMPREHENSIVE WIRELESS

F110 is built to meet all your mobile wireless needs, with higher speed and more power efficiency. Wi-Fi 6E and an optional 5G Sub-6 capabilities deliver precise navigation and ample throughput while supporting 4G LTE module with an integrated GPS providing coverage for most regions, or dedicated GPS is also available. Bluetooth 5.3 handles low-power Internet of things (IoT) connections, with RF passthrough for GPS, WWAN, and WLAN, all controllable using our proprietary G-Manager software.

SUPERB OUTDOOR MEDIA CAPABILITIES

F110’s 11.6-inch touchscreen display delivers a brilliant 1,200 nits of screen brightness, enabling clear outdoor reading, video, and AR overlay on even the brightest days. Its five and eight (front/rear) megapixel cameras provide image and clarity.
